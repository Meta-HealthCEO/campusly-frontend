Analytics & BI — Features

  • Revenue trend with AI forecast — actual bars transition to dashed forecast bars, powered by the Python analytics engine
  • Cash flow forecasting — projected inflows vs outflows for the next 30 days
  • Customer insights — new vs returning, avg basket size, churn risk detection
  • AI recommendations — actionable cards: pricing optimization, stock alerts, staffing suggestions
  • Top products — revenue-ranked product table
  • Revenue heatmap — day x hour grid showing peak trading times at a glance
  • All data filterable by date range and payment channel
  • Export — PDF reports, CSV data exports, scheduled email reports
