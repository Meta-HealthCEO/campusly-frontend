POS — Key Features

  • PWA — works on tablets, laptops, phones. Offline-capable with sync
  • Product grid with category tabs, search, custom items, stock counts
  • Cart panel — quantity controls, modifiers (size, extras), item notes
  • Customer linking — attach to existing customer for loyalty & history
  • All payment methods — Card, EFT, Cash, Google Pay, Apple Pay, Crypto via One Gate gateway
  • Loyalty discounts auto-applied based on customer history
  • Split payments — split across multiple methods
  • Hold orders — park and recall later
  • Inventory auto-deduct — stock levels update in real-time
  • Receipt — digital (email/SMS) or connected printer
