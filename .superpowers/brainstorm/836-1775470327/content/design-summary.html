Key Behaviours

  • Click a book cover → navigates directly to the textbook reader
  • Click a list row → also navigates to the reader
  • Shelf view filters: Search, Framework, Subject, Status (no Grade — shelves ARE the grades)
  • List view filters: Search, Framework, Subject, Grade, Status (all filters shown)
  • Book cover colours: auto-assigned by subject name from a fixed palette
  • Shelf = wooden background with horizontal scroll if many books per grade
  • Chapter count shown below each book cover
  • View toggle persists in localStorage
