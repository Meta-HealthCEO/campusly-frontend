# 10 — Learning — Phase 2

## Current State
Study materials, quizzes with auto-grading, rubrics, assignment submissions, student progress analytics.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. AI-Powered Content Recommendations
- Based on quiz results and weak topics, suggest relevant study materials
- "You scored low on fractions — here are 3 resources to help"
- **Why:** Personalized learning paths without requiring teacher intervention. Scales support to every student

### 2. Interactive Quizzes
- Timer mode for exam simulation
- Instant feedback after each question (not just at the end)
- Retry mode for practice
- **Why:** Static quizzes are 2010. Interactive quizzes with immediate feedback improve retention significantly

### 3. Study Material Organization
- Folder/collection system so teachers can group materials into units or topics
- Student bookmarking/favorites
- **Why:** A flat list of materials becomes unusable at 50+ items. Organization is essential

### 4. Progress-Based Learning Paths
- Unlock next material/quiz only after achieving a threshold on the previous
- Visual progress bar per subject
- **Why:** Guided progression keeps students on track and gives a sense of accomplishment

### 5. Virtual Classroom Integration
- See [31-virtual-classroom.md](31-virtual-classroom.md) for the full virtual classroom module
- Recorded live sessions auto-appear in the study materials library
- Video watch progress tracked alongside quiz/assignment progress
- Teachers can require "watch this lesson" as a prerequisite for a quiz
