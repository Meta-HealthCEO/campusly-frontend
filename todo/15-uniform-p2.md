# 15 — Uniform — Phase 2

## Current State
Catalog, orders, second-hand marketplace, size guides.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. Online Payment for Orders
- Integrate with payment gateway (same as fees) for uniform purchases
- Order confirmation email with expected collection date
- **Why:** Parents should be able to order and pay from home, not queue at the school shop

### 2. Order Notifications
- Status updates: order received → ready for collection → collected
- Push/SMS at each stage
- **Why:** "Is my child's blazer ready?" calls clog the school office. Notifications eliminate them

### 3. Second-Hand Marketplace Improvements
- Photo upload for listed items
- Condition grading (new, like new, good, fair)
- In-app messaging between buyer and seller
- **Why:** A proper marketplace reduces uniform costs for families and reduces waste. Win-win
