# 18 — Transport — Phase 2

## Current State
Bus routes, student assignments, boarding logs with GPS, transport alerts.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. Live Bus Tracking
- Real-time map showing bus position (GPS from driver's phone)
- ETA to each stop
- Parent can see "bus is 5 minutes away" on their dashboard
- **Why:** This is the transport feature. Parents standing at the stop in the rain wondering where the bus is — solved

### 2. Boarding Notifications
- Push to parent when child boards and when child alights
- **Why:** Peace of mind. "Your child has been dropped off safely" is worth the entire platform subscription to a parent

### 3. Route Capacity Management
- Visual indicator of seats filled vs. available per route
- Waitlist when a route is full
- Admin alert when route is oversubscribed
- **Why:** Overcrowded buses are a safety and legal issue. Capacity visibility prevents it
