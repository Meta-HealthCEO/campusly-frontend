# 14 — Sport — Phase 2

## Current State
Teams, fixtures, results, MVP voting, season standings.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. Fixture Reminders & Results Push
- Notification to parents and players before fixtures (day before + 2 hours before)
- Auto-push results to parents after match result is captured
- **Why:** Sports parents live for match results. Instant notification beats the WhatsApp group

### 2. Player Statistics
- Per-player: appearances, goals/points, man of the match awards per season
- Profile card showing career stats
- **Why:** Kids are motivated by their stats. Parents love sharing them. Coaches use them for selection

### 3. Live Fixture Calendar
- Calendar view of all upcoming fixtures filterable by sport/team
- Subscribe via iCal feed
- **Why:** "When is the next rugby match?" should be answered by the app, not a circular letter
