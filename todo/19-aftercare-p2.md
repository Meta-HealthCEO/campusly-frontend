# 19 — Aftercare — Phase 2

## Current State
Registration, attendance, pickup authorization, activities, monthly invoicing.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. Late Pickup Alerts
- If child not collected by authorized time, auto-escalate: SMS to primary parent → secondary parent → emergency contacts
- Log of late pickups per student
- **Why:** Children waiting uncollected is a safeguarding concern. Escalation protects the child and documents the pattern

### 2. Pickup Notification
- Push to parent when child is signed out and by whom
- Photo of authorized person displayed to aftercare staff for verification
- **Why:** Knowing your child was collected safely, and by whom, is basic peace of mind

### 3. Activity Booking
- Parents view available aftercare activities and register their child
- Capacity limits per activity
- **Why:** Currently activities are admin-managed. Self-service booking reduces admin work and gives parents choice
