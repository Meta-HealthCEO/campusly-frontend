# 29 — AI Tools — Phase 2

## Current State
AI exam paper generation (CAPS-aligned), question regeneration, AI-assisted grading with rubrics.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. AI Tutor (see also: todo/ai-tutor.md)
- Student-facing AI that helps understand test results and prepare for assessments
- Generates practice problems based on syllabus and weak areas
- Walk-through mode with step-by-step explanations
- **Why:** This is the feature that puts Campusly in a different league. Personalized tutoring at scale is transformative for SA education

### 2. Paper PDF Export
- Generate formatted PDF of exam paper + separate memorandum PDF
- School branding, correct formatting for printing
- **Why:** Teachers generate papers in the app but need to print them. PDF export closes the loop

### 3. AI Usage Dashboard
- Per-school: AI calls used, tokens consumed, papers generated, submissions graded
- Quota management per subscription tier
- **Why:** AI costs money. Schools on lower tiers need limits; premium schools get more. Without tracking, costs spiral

### 4. Student Grade Visibility
- After AI grading, teacher reviews and approves → grades visible to student
- Student sees per-criterion scores and AI feedback
- **Why:** AI grades that only teachers see are only half the value. Students need the feedback to learn

### 5. Paper Version History
- Track regenerated questions, keep original and revised versions
- Teacher can revert individual questions
- **Why:** "I liked the previous version of question 3" — without history, it's gone forever
