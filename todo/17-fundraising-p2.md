# 17 — Fundraising — Phase 2

## Current State
Campaigns, donations, raffles, tax certificates, donor wall, recurring donations.

## Phase 2 Enhancements

### 1. Online Donation Payments
- Integrated payment gateway for card/EFT donations
- One-click donate from campaign page
- Auto-receipt email on donation
- **Why:** If donating requires an EFT and a reference number, you lose 80% of donors. One-click changes everything

### 2. Campaign Progress Visualization
- Progress bar with real-time amount raised vs. target
- Milestone celebrations (50%, 75%, 100%)
- Social sharing when milestones hit
- **Why:** Visible progress motivates more donations. People want to be part of something that's winning

### 3. Automated Tax Certificates
- Auto-generate Section 18A certificate when donation is confirmed
- Email to donor with PDF attachment
- **Why:** Manual certificate generation is slow and error-prone. Automation makes donors happy and keeps the school compliant
